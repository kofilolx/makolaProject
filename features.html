Key Features

Ingredient Selection

Users can select from a variety of common ingredients used in Ghanaian cooking. This feature ensures that conversions are specific to the selected ingredient, providing more accurate results.

Unit Conversion

The converter allows users to select different units of measurement, such as weight in kilograms or the local unit "olonka." This feature provides flexibility for users who may use different measurement systems.

Custom Conversion Factor Submission

Users can submit their own conversion factors for ingredients not already included in the system. This feature allows the application to grow and adapt to the needs of its users, ensuring it remains relevant and useful.
